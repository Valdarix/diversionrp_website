Want to Support Us?

Supporters in our Discord community are valued members who help keep our server running through their generous donations. By subscribing via Discord Subscriptions, Supporters gain access to exclusive Discord channels, special roles, unique emojis, and become members of our steering committee. We offer three tiers of Supporter status, allowing members to show their support at a level that fits their budget. While these contributions are 100% optional and not expected, they are deeply appreciated. We use Discord for subscriptions instead of Tebex because it is easier to manage when there are no in-city benefits, and it is important to note that there are no in-city benefits or in-game advantages tied to this status. We are committed to ensuring that all in-game content and features remain free and accessible to everyone. Your support helps enhance our community and maintain our server, and we are deeply grateful for your contributions.
