About our city

Diversion RP is a new city with a big goal, be your last stop on your journey to find your forever home.
Supported by a engaged and caring owner, our dedicated staff team and developers are working hard to bring you the best roleplay experience possible.

We are a small city with a big goal, and we are looking for you to join us.

Features

Our city focused is on roleplay first! All of our scripts, assets, and decisions are based around that core principal.

  • Roleplay!
  • Whitelisted!
  • Add-on cars available to all!
  • Housing open to all!
  • LEO & EMS RP!
  • Lots of clothing and hair options for male and female!
  • Own a Business!
  • Run a Gang, MC, or Family!

Needs

Every city has needs, and as a new city we are no different. Our growing city is looking for the following:

  • LEO! Our LSPD department is the first department in the city and looking for officers of all ranks.
  • EMS! Our dedicated EMS chief is looking for more to join his team.
  • Business Owners! Active business owners are always welcome.
  • YOU!!!!!!!!

Technical

We are focused on giving our players a fun but stable city to play in.

  • OPTIMIZED!!!! We make all efforts to ensure all add-on assets are optimized for performance.
  • Dedicated server hardware on a reputable hosting provider.
  • Routine backups of code, database, and assets.
  • Professional and experienced developers!
