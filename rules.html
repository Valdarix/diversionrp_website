Rules & Guidelines

Before applying to join our city on Discord, you must review these community guidelines in full. Your application will include questions to verify your understanding of these rules. Please note that claiming ignorance ("I didn't know") will not excuse rule violations.

Values

ROLEPLAY

We expect all community members to roleplay to the highest possible standard and remain in character while in-game. A person must act in the confines of the character they have created and are currently playing as, if you wish to switch characters you must log out of the server and log back in as your new character.


RESPECT & DIVERSITY

All community members must be respectful at all times while in-city or on our community platforms. This means that users must never be involved with or publish any content that is deemed racist, homophobic, sexist, prejudiced, or disrespectful. We also forbid the sharing or publishing of any sexually explicit content on any of our community platforms.


FUN

DRP strives to create a city where everyone can enjoy their time. This city and community are meant to be places that everyone can enjoy their off time. At the end of the day, this is a game and we want people to enjoy their time playing it.

Section 1: General Rules

Communication

You must be able to understand, speak, and type English since we are a North American based server.

You must have a working microphone. DRP is an all-inclusive community that includes people with disabilities. If you have a disability that hinders your ability to use a microphone, please reach out to a member of the staff to facilitate another method of communication.

Respect for the Community

Please carefully observe the following guidelines to ensure a respectful and harmonious environment:


  • Respectful Communication on Official Platforms: Communications related to DRP, whether in-game or on official DRP platforms, including streamer channels, must always be respectful. Refrain from sending angry or offensive messages resulting from in-game events.
  • In-Character Interaction: We understand that in-character (IC) interaction can include trash talk and abrasive behavior as part of the role-playing experience. This is acceptable as long as it remains in character and does not breach the boundaries of respect or target players out of character (OOC).
  • Boundary Between IC and OOC: Any behavior that crosses from in-character to out-of-character, especially if it becomes disrespectful or offensive, is not permissible. Maintaining this distinction is crucial to ensure that in-game dynamics do not lead to personal conflicts or breaches of our community standards.
  • Private Interactions: Direct messages (DMs) between individuals, if not involving official DRP platforms, are generally beyond our scope, respecting your privacy in personal matters. However, extreme cases such as stalking or sexually explicit content, if reported and impacting in-city dynamics, will be addressed firmly.
  • Disputes with Staff: If you disagree with a staff ruling, please address it constructively. Instead of direct confrontation, submit a ticket for a formal review of the issue. This approach promotes a more effective resolution process.
  • No Trolling: Engaging in behavior intended to provoke or disturb players, members, police, staff, etc., under any circumstances, is strictly prohibited.

Your commitment to these guidelines is essential. They are designed not only to maintain order but to enhance everyone's gaming experience.

Administrator Instructions/Rulings

If an administrator's ruling seems to have been made in error the dispute must be handled outside the game via discord by creating a staff complaint ticket. Please do not "DM" members of the Staff Team directly, use the proper channel within Discord, follow the proper chain of command or open a support ticket. Do not privately message the owner of the city to circumvent a disciplinary action.

Bans

While you are banned from the server and/or Discord you are not allowed to register another account or use an alternate account until the ban period has expired. Otherwise, your other account may also be banned and your original ban extended. You may appeal bans by creating a ticket in the CrazySwitch Discord.

Game Modifications

You are not permitted to use a modification that grants you an advantage on the server. DO NOT install game mods that alter vehicle handling, performance, or that can gain you any type of advantage over others. Cheats, mods, plugins, scripts, or any third-party additions will get you banned if you are caught using them. Our server uses a 3rd party Anti-Cheat that can also get you banned from other servers.

Recruitment/Advertising Other Servers/Self-Promotion

Absolutely NO recruiting to other servers or advertising other servers in any DRP discord or in game. Discord server profile “about me” section must not advertise other servers. Your main Discord “about me” can be whatever you’d like but the profile for any DRP discord needs to not advertise to other servers. No self-promoting, promoting other servers, or recruitment is allowed. This includes Direct messaging or spamming invites to other members.

Section 2: Server Rules

Going Out of Character (OOC)


You must always strive to RP first before ever going OOC. Please, do not use the OOC to end a scene, try to change the scene using RP first. Ending a scene by saying or going OOC is considered FailRP. Going OOC during RP scenes can result in disciplinary action.


If you need a staff member:

- If a staff member is available in-game, doing /report can get you assistance. Doing /report does not guarantee that you will get help right that moment.

- If you have an issue with how RP is going, the best way to get a staff member is to create a ticket in the discord.

Deathmatching

Killing another player for little or no engaged roleplay is strictly forbidden. There will be absolutely no RDM or VDM. (For Example: If a traffic accident has occurred while a player was hit by a car it needs to be roleplayed out.)

Downed & New Life Rule

  • Phase 1: (On your side)

    - When you are in this phase you are considered knocked out/down, which means you're able to still speak in game and on radio

    • - You can remember the general interaction that lead to you ending up on the ground. You still are required to act hurt as you have been injured.

  • Phase 2: (On your back)

    - If you see the light, you must forget everything within the past 30 minutes . (from the time you go on your back and 30 minutes prior).

    - You may be reminded of things that happened prior to seeing the light by someone who was involved on the scene. Due to EMS federal protection they can only tell you what procedures were done to you. They must not tell you who brought you or who shot you if they know. EMS must remain neutral at all times.

Do Not Return to the Scene

If you are downed or removed from the scene by EMS/Fire, Police, or taken to the hospital by another subject you are not allowed to return to the scene until the situation/scene is complete.

Calling for EMS

If you are down for any reason, EMS is to be utilized when they are available, but if they are busy or at a different location, give them a reasonable amount of time to arrive at your location or at the hospital.

Power-Gaming

Power-gaming is the act of using an in-game mechanic, any external information, or a roleplay concept to favor your own character or story, and give yourself an unfair or unrealistic advantage. (No running to teleportation markers to escape, such as running into your apartment or anywhere that is not accessible by others.


This includes an example of doing a robbery, getting in a chase and running to a garage, and putting your vehicle away.


If you are still in your original vehicle then there must be a 10-minute wait period to store that vehicle, repair, modify or pull another vehicle after a pursuit.) Another example of powergaming would be kidnapping a cop to get your friend out of jail.


The reason it is seen as powergaming is because LEO’s have to value the hostages life and negotiate.


There are ways to break your friends out of jail other than kidnapping a cop.

Also, If you are asked for your federal ID in-game, you are required to give it for reporting purposes or face disciplinary actions!

Metagaming

The act of using out-of-character/external information for in-game situations that you may become a part of. This includes stream sniping, 3rd party VOIP applications (discord calls, TS for non-LEO), etc. Any information used from watching streams, typing/reading chat, etc. will be met with disciplinary action up to and/or including a suspension from the server.

Offensive RP/Suicide RP/Combat Logging

  • Roleplaying any type of sexual assault such as any non-consensual touching, rape, etc. is not allowed and will result in a disciplinary action, and an appeal will not be allowed.
  • Suicide RP will not be accepted or tolerated.
  • Roleplaying a mental Illness will not be accepted or tolerated.
  • Logging off or forcibly re-spawning to avoid situations is not allowed. IF you have to log off but are in the middle of an RP situation, you will need acknowledgment from all parties involved. This includes police chases and/or medical scenes. If you know you do not have the time to complete a scenario, do not start it. If all parties involved do not agree that the scene must be ended and you log out it will be considered combat logging and be dealt with accordingly.
  • Once you commit a crime you must wait ten minutes before logging out of the server. If you are in the middle of a scene and your game crashes or something causes you to suddenly leave, try to log back in as soon as possible and explain your situation. If you cannot get back in within 5 minutes, please create a ticket and explain what happened and whom you were involved with during your scene so we can notify them of the issue. Abusing this rule will result in a disciplinary action.

Zero-Tolerance Rule

DRP has ZERO tolerance for ANY sexual misconduct, not following this rule will result in disciplinary action. Any Reports of Sexual harassment of any degree at any time will be handled accordingly and investigated thoroughly by the Senior Administration. Rape scenes of any nature are PROHIBITED, even with consent from other players this behavior will get you both banned without the ability to appeal.


Roleplaying sexual assault (such as non-consensual touching, rape, etc.) is not allowed and will result in a disciplinary action and will be thoroughly investigated by Senior Administration. We do not tolerate this behavior.


Racial Slurs/slang words and Transphobic/Homophobic terms used in any way towards others will not be accepted or tolerated. We do not take these situations lightly. Each report will be determined on a case by case basis.


Additionally, the use of directed sexually explicit insults, which includes any language intended to sexually degrade, shame, or insult a specific individual, is strictly prohibited. General use of sexual terms in a non-targeted manner is permissible within the context of the game. However, using such language to personally attack, demean, or harass another player is unacceptable and will be subject to severe disciplinary action.

Erotic Roleplay

Any and all sexually explicit or nude activities must be conducted in the privacy of your own apartment or home. These activities may not take place in public spaces and are strictly prohibited while streaming on any platform.


Additionally, any discussion, implication, or reference to sexual activity is prohibited on in-city social media apps.

Exploiting Bugs

At no time are you allowed to exploit bugs in-game for personal gain. If it’s too good to be true, it probably is. Please report a bug if you find one via a discord ticket.

Interactions between your characters

You are allowed to have multiple characters in the city, however, your personal characters cannot know each other from an in-character perspective. You are also not allowed to interact between the two characters ie. transferring money or any other assets between your characters or using a third-party "friend" to transfer items between your characters. If this is discovered those items will be deleted from your inventory and you will receive disciplinary action.

Robbing Players

When robbing another player, you are only able to take what they currently have in their inventory.


You Cannot:

  • Remove Phones, Food Items, Radios
  • Take items from someone else's apartment.
  • Request bank transfers or force them to sell their car(s)
  • In the instance that you are robbing/trying to kidnap a player, you cannot kill that player unless they outright refuse to comply, provide resistance, or provoke you to inflict harm.
  • When robbing businesses you can only take what is in the workers pockets, not their stashes or boss menu accounts.

If a person is killed (on their side or back) they can be robbed. However, the reason for killing them can not be specifically to rob them. There must be roleplay behind it.

Healing in a situation

All citizens are allowed to use bandages and/or add armor during situations, including drugs.

Exploiting Scripts/GTA V Mechanics

Knowingly exploiting the script and/or the GTA V engine in order to gain an advantage is not allowed. This includes abusing the physics of GTA V in order to do unrealistic things such as ramping cars off cliffs and continuing to flee. Unrealistic driving is considered FailRP.


You need to roleplay out vehicle damage. Examples include:

  • If you hit a pole head on with significant front end damage, your car would be totaled.
  • If you are going 75 mph and hit a barrier or pole or some heavy object multiple times the natural RP would be damaging your vehicle to the point it is undriveable. Not continuing to drive after striking multiple barriers and other things at high speeds.
  • Driving in the city limits constantly at 100mph+ is also unrealistic.
  • Do not use emotes to avoid fall damage, enter locked areas, pull people out of locked areas or anything that would provide an unrealistic advantage.

Failure to Roleplay/Unrealistic RP

Failing to roleplay is strictly forbidden. DRP is a semi-realistic roleplay server, you are expected to be roleplaying at all times. If a situation presents itself where rules are broken, roleplay first, and report later. You are expected to use script features to assist in roleplay. DO NOT continuously type in OOC chat while another player is trying to roleplay with you.

Safe Zones

Safe Zones must be crime-free. It is FailRP to run away from a crime and enter a safe zone. If the RP begins outside of the safe zone and is brought into the safe zone you may continue the criminal RP, however, it must be realistic. If you are loitering in a safe-zone your federal protection is removed.


The following are considered safe zones unless the RP has led there:

  • Inside and car ports of Police Stations
  • EMS/FIRE Stations
  • Inside Hospitals (Inside or under awning) (no retaliatory crimes)
  • Spawn locations are SAFE ZONES in that a player must be allowed to move freely from the spawn location after flying in and have two minutes to collect themselves and their vehicle. Spawn locations are not protected when loitering in the area.

Impersonating Emergency Personnel

Citizens are prohibited from impersonating emergency personnel this includes, but is not limited to, Police, Fire/EMS, DOT, and Communications.

Stealing or Harming Federally Protected Vehicles and/or Personnel

Personnel:

  • EMS/FIRE and Communications employees are federally protected. Kidnapping, Killing, Robbing, or holding hostage EMS/Fire or Communication employees is prohibited.
  • In the case of a scene with active shooting:
  • If EMS is staged near a scene it can continue, but if EMS enters the scene, all criminal activity within the view of EMS should cease once EMS arrives. EMS must not be used as a means to end a scene and must only be called in once it is cleared by Police.

Vehicles:

  • Citizens are not allowed to steal LEO/EMS/Fire/SADOT vehicles. You may steal from police vehicles.
  • Fire/EMS vehicles/personnel are restricted from being stolen and stolen from.
  • All citizens are not allowed to steal local and business aircraft, local EMS, or local military vehicles. (Vehicles include helicopters, tanks, jets, transport vehicles, cargo bobs, military helicopters, etc.)

Robbery Rules

Hostages:

  • There must be a minimum of 6 officers for high priority crime. (i.e. bank robbery, jewelry store robbery. Except for taking a police hostage where there must be 7 officers on duty.)
  • Robbers can not take a friend(s) hostage, one of the robbers cannot pose as a hostage or have a fake hostage. The Robbers must go out and find a random person and take them hostage for these scenarios.
  • There is no limit on hostages, however there must be a reasonable amount of individuals to manage the amount of hostages you have.
  • Taking the hostage with you after the crime is committed is not allowed, a hostage will not be allowed to leave the scene of a robbery. Law Enforcement will take action to prevent the hostage from being taken from the scene.
  • If a ransom is being requested in exchange for the hostage, the hostage MUST be exchanged for the money. (Total ransom allowed for request: $25,000 per hostage in cash.)

Robbers:

  • No more than 5 robbers and 1 lookout on the scene of a robbery.
  • No more than 3 vehicles will be allowed
  • Your scene must be set up before starting the robbery. You can not start the robbery and then add people to it or show up later with the hostage.
  • Robbers can ask for demands during negotiations (cops not pursuing and taking the hostage from the scene is not a demand option).

LEO:

  • Law enforcement will begin conducting the negotiations and are allowed a plus 2 rule.
  • In the event that an officer is shot, an all call may be used to call in all available officers with no limit to how many respond.
  • Negotiations can be conducted over a mutual radio channel between robbers and police, by phone or with a megaphone.

Cop Baiting

No cop baiting. Do not purposefully try and get police attention on you by doing things you would not do with an Officer near you. This includes burning out in the middle of intersections, purposefully revving your motor at lights to bait a pullover/chase, and purposely committing traffic violations in the vicinity of police.

EMS Authority over Respawn/EMS Custody/Injuries after EMS Care

Respawn:

  • When in EMS custody, do not respawn (see the light) without permission. When you are revived, you are in medical custody until the medic clears you.

Police Mistreatment and Appeal

If you feel you have been mistreated in a police situation, use the proper channels in city (ie. speak to a supervisor, fill out a complaint form for that specific agency). Do not contact an admin unless the officer is clearly breaking a server rule. Take abuse of power as in character and report it as you see fit.

RDM/VDM

Deadly action needs to have RP behind it.

  • Random Deathmatch (RDM): killing/attempting to kill without intent and roleplay. Killing on sight without proper escalation is not allowed as it could be seen as a form of RDM. Proper escalation would be an ongoing scene that would allow shooting on sight. (i.e Interactions between individuals or groups of individuals that have escalated to violence.)
  • Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM): using a vehicle to kill/attempt to kill without intent or roleplay.

When to put in a ticket/report a player:

  • Example, if someone hits you with their car and you are unhappy, find that person and role play out getting insurance, payback, etc.
  • If you are run over multiple times and put on your back then escalate to a ticket.

Fear RP

Fear RP is what you would do in real life if faced with a scary situation, it is an RP server do not treat it like a videogame. You must fear for your life, just as you would in a normal situation outside of the game.

  • For example, if you are alone in an alleyway and individuals hold you at gunpoint and you cannot escape without dying, you must follow FearRP. The average person would follow their demands as they are helpless in the situation, you wouldn't just walk away or try to pull out a weapon when you are outnumbered.
  • If you are in your car and someone pulls a gun on you, it is acceptable to speed away, but you are taking a chance on being shot/shot at.
  • If the cop/suspect informs you to not touch the radio/phone this includes hitting your panic button you must comply or risk being killed.

Perma-Death/Death Penalty

A player may decide to perm-death any of their characters. No player will be forced to perm-death a character, except in the following instances

  • The administration team may choose to give your character a Death Penalty if you are considered a repeat offender of deadly crimes or show blatant toxicity with your character’s roleplay.
  • You do not have to report that you are killing off a character.

If a player decides to do a perm-death, they must be declared dead by any on-duty EMS, use the "/me" command to inform the EMS of your intentions when they arrive on the scene.

Scene Limits

The maximum number of civilians per scene is 4 civilians per criminal scene.

Torture RP

While Torture RP is allowed it must be done within reason and the victim must be allowed to continue to RP after the scenario with all body parts still functional and intact if they choose to do so. They can keep any deformities left from the RP if they wish. SEXUAL ACTS ARE NOT ALLOWED.

Camping Illicit Areas

Illicit areas include drugs areas, job areas, money washing, etc.

  • Civilians cannot intentionally sit in high traffic areas that lead to illicit spots for the reason of robbing individuals.
  • Cops are also not allowed to camp drug areas for the sole purpose of making a traffic stop and busting the subject leaving the drug area. Officers are allowed to conduct surveillance to help build a bigger case.

False Flag Operations by Civilians

Civilians who execute actions with the clear intent of conducting a false flag operation are subject to death from the gang they are impersonating. For example, robbing a bank and wearing other gangs’ EUP with the intent to get them blamed for your actions.

Respect For Life Rule

Any situation leading to a shootout or a killing must have reasonable RP behind it. You MUST attempt to RP the scenario before going straight into violence.


There is a shooting cooldown, regardless of scene type, you must wait 30 minutes before initiating another shootout. Roleplay over Gunplay is HIGHLY recommended!

Server Restart Rule

ANO criminal Activity is to happen 30 minutes before and/or after a Server Restart. This is everyone's time to put their belongings away, put vehicles in garage. Restart times are 2:00am, 6:00am, 5:00pm EST.

Reporting Processes

DRP is not a ticket based city. However, with rules comes rule breaks and with rule breaks comes reports. In order to properly fill out a ticket to report a player these are the processes.

  • You must submit all evidence you have of the situation in either screenshots or clips. Clips must be unedited and must include sound. If you have to submit a lot of clips that is OK.
  • Clips must have 2 minutes prior to the reported incident and 2 minutes after.
  • Tickets with no evidence will be dismissed and should be handled in the city through RP.
  • You should have all of your evidence ready to go and ready to add detail to your ticket when you open it.
  • If you suspect a rule break you need to include the rule from the rule book that you believe was broken with the evidence.

Animal RP

The usage of animal peds would require the following steps for approval:

  • RP Support Ticket opened
  • Backstory regarding the animal they are wanting to use
  • Explanation of the Roleplay behind the use of the ped
  • After all information Senior Admin role pinged for approval.
  • Standing approval for the usage of that specific ped no others.
  • All roleplay must fall in line with realistic animal rp.
  • You are not allowed to RP LEO Dogs

Third Partying

Involving yourself in a scene that is on-going. The only exception to this rule is if you are running from the police and you wreck your vehicle you can call for a pick-up. If the original scene doesn't involve you, do not involve yourself.

Dirty Cop RP

Joining a department within the city is a privilege and must be treated as such. Dirty cop RP will not be tolerated. If you are caught being a dirty cop your discord ID will be blacklisted from being accepted into any LEO department in the server.

Section 3: VIP Rules

Sale of VIP cars

The sale of VIP cars is prohibited. Please do not sell your vehicle or VIP benefits to any other player. You may give or trade your vehicle at your discretion and are subject to losing the vehicle forever and will not be a reportable offense.

Section 4: Disciplinary Actions

Written Warning

The first level of disciplinary action at DRP is a written warning

Community Suspension

The second level of disciplinary action at DRP is a Community Suspension

  • The suspension option will depend on your actions warranting the suspension, which can be: 24 - 72 hours
  • During this period you will still be able to participate in discord conversations, however, you will not be allowed on the server.

Extended Community Suspension

The third level of disciplinary action at DRP is an Extended Community Suspension

  • The suspension option will depend on your actions warranting the suspension, which can be 36 hours - 7 days, determined by the staff team on a case-by-case basis.

Permanent Removal from the Community

The last level of disciplinary action at DRP is Removal from the community. After multiple attempts to correct actions or a first-time serious offense, you have shown that you lack the maturity to participate in RP in our community. You will be permanently removed from all community platforms.


Depending on the situation you may be able to re-apply after 60 days.

Section 5: Gang RP

YOU CAN ONLY HAVE ONE GANG CHARACTER AT A TIME


Gang Definition

The definition of gang includes but is not limited to members that share an identity, typically linked to a name, and often other symbols. Members that view themselves as a gang, have some permanence and a degree of organization and are involved in an elevated level of criminal activity.

Gang Hopping and Limits

  • There is a 7 day cool down between leaving and joining a gang.
  • If a gang is caught breaking said rule they are susceptible to receiving a strike.
  • Players that are gang switching without following this rule could face disciplinary action

Exceptions:

  • Gang trading is acceptable and you are not subject to the 7 day cool down.
  • If you are in a gang that gets disbanded then you may join another gang and it does not subject you to the 7 day cool down.

Blood In / Blood Out

Gang membership follows the “blood in/blood” out rule. Entering a gang fully exposes the member to the gang's rules and processes. It is up to the gang leadership to make clear what the terms are for leaving or being bled out. Members may be allowed to leave on good terms determined by the remaining gang members; however, if the character is found to violate the terms of release. A bleed out will be subject to the gang’s processes in their by-laws.

New Gangs

All new gangs will be considered unrecognized and must have approval to run as an unrecognized gang from the government. They will be under a 14-day probationary period where they will only receive 2 strikes before disbandment. While under the 14-day probation, new gangs are allowed to do all gang-related activities except gang wars and gang beef.

Section 6: General Gang Rules

Gang Membership, Minimums and Establishment

Establishing an organization follows a few specific steps:


The first is completing the IC via finding Franklin’s bodyguard to allow you to see Franklin (use your resources and connections IC for more information).


  • There must be a minimum of 5 full members (not hang-arounds or prospects) in the gang before you can create an unrecognized group.
  • You must maintain 5 members to remain a group. A grace period of 1 week will be given to get back to the correct number of members before the group is disbanded.
  • There is a maximum of 25 members per organization.
  • Once this person is found, groups looking to be recognized must ensure they have a valid reputation within the city and be brought to The Elite Order from an already Recognized organization.
  • Once your organization meets with The Elite Order, they will provide you with next step instructions depending on how the meeting goes.

If your organization is accepted, there will be one final meeting where The Elite Order will provide with you a final piece to Franklin's puzzle to create your organization.


  • Once created, you will enter a 2-week “learning” period in which your organization is meant to learn our system, how our team operates, and expectations of organizations.
  • After this “Learning Period” your organization will be eligible to apply for their Elite status if you wish to do so.
  • Any group can be made without an Elite status and utilize the gang system to its fullest potential immediately, Elite status is not required at any point but provides extra benefits.

If your organization receives Elite status, you will enter a 2-week “Trial Period” in which you are monitored more closely and should remain in good standing, abide by server rules, choose roleplay over gunplay, etc.


  • After this 2-week “Trial Period” you will be up for review. If the review goes well you will be deemed fully Elite and be eligible to purchase gang packages for the import of clothing, cars, MLO, etc.
  • The leader of the organization requesting elite status must open a gang ticket and confirm name, roster and logo.
  • Gang staff will discuss and go over that information within 48 hours.

If your organization is denied Elite status, you will have to wait 7 days before having another sit down with The Elite Order. However, you will be able to remain as an unofficial group IC. Your focus should be providing quality roleplay for the server.

Elite Status

Elite status can be pulled at any given time at staff discretion. Our Elite gangs are held to a higher standard and are there to act as a role model for the server.


Elite Organizations can:


  • Purchase a custom MLO/Compound to be placed on your turf. (Request in a ticket to confirm the MLO and price which is subject to change)(if open)
  • The leader of the organization requesting elite status must open a gang ticket and confirm name, roster and logo.
  • Gang staff will discuss and go over that information within 48 hours.

All benefits are subject to removal if Elite status is revoked.

  • Going under the 5 member minimum will revoke your Elite status.

Gangs/Families/MCs are allowed to own 1 business within their entire group.

  • Must comply with all business rules and representation of your organization on the business property. Review business rules for more information on this.

General Guidelines

Gangs are still expected to follow all DRP server rules. This includes LEO interactions. Any criminal action in or outside of the gang turf must still adhere to all the DRP General Rules.

Each gang leader is expected to make sure that their gang members are following server rules

Gang Colors

Gangs cannot dictate the color of mask, clothing or cars to any player.

Only exception is if someone is found to be wearing a specific gang EUP.

Gang Colors

Gangs that control a block are allowed to chase out any unwanted persons from the territory that are posing a threat to the gang and are allowed to protect their territory from rival gangs except for when safe passage must be allowed.

  • Safe passage to critical in-game assets must be allowed for civilians if no other alternative to that asset exists in the city.
  • The use of vehicle sales locations, and supply stores (food or utility) must not be hindered. This includes gas stations in your area.
  • Drug locations are NOT Critical In game assets.
  • If civilians are caught selling drugs or robbing stores within gang territory by the gang that controls the area, they are subject to taxes, threats, and up to violence.
  • Gangs are allowed to first press then escalate to violence if a civilian is seen wearing their gang bandana.
  • At any time a civilian is in a gang territory causing trouble there will not be a limit of gang members that can protect the territory.
  • Gangs cannot chase out people that are simply roleplaying as a civilian not doing criminal RP.
  • You may press other gang members in your territory if they have other options to obtain said asset.
  • Gang members cannot rob civilians. If you are a hangaround for a gang you are not considered a civilian anymore and can be robbed. If you are a civilian hanging out with criminals/gang and you have now put yourself out there to be robbed, shot at or killed.

Law Enforcement/ EMS Interactions

Violence must be the final step when interacting with cops that inject into gang on gang activity

  • Safe passage to critical in-game assets must be allowed for civilians if no other alternative to that asset exists in the city. Police must not be interfered with while conducting police business (i.e.. traffic stops) unless the situation involves your gang as a whole.
  • Law enforcement will be trained to not shoot on sight, the same rules must be followed by the gangs as well..
  • You cannot make police leave and you must fear RP if police arrive and scatter.
  • EMS will not be a gang's personal revive bot. Should your character be injured or go down in a gang-on-gang situation, EMS will not come on the scene until the end of the RP. If the RP is in a red zone (trap houses, drug processing, etc.), EMS will not respond.

Penalizations & Discipline for Gangs

  • 1st Strike - $250k in city fine paid to The Elite Order and a warning that will allow the gang to make adjustments.
  • 2nd Strike - $500k in city fine paid to The Elite Order and the gang will be placed on suspension (see the section below).
  • 3rd Strike - $1mil in city fine paid to The Elite Order and the gang will risk being disbanded or incurring other punishments.
  • Gang Strikes will be removed after 30 days but if gangs are repeating the same behavior strikes may not be removed.

Suspension Notes

While we reserve the right to suspend and/or disband your gang if deemed necessary, following the guidelines above, a gang may be placed on suspension.

  • During this suspension time, your gang is to strip all EUP and follow civilian rules for criminal RP.
  • Gang activity is defined by more than 4 members committing crime together, occupying the same area, or being in radio.
  • Suspension rules will be strictly enforced. Deviating from suspension rules could lead to the disbandment of your gang

Gang Rivalries

  • Gang Rivalries are not war.
  • Simple disrespect between gangs must NOT lead to an escalation higher than disrespect (trash-talk) without prior escalation. (i.e you cannot go straight to shooting.)
  • Rivalries must be had before war is started.
  • You must always RP before starting a rivalry. Rivalries can be initiated via drive-bys in opposing gang territories.
  • You are subject to police interactions, you cannot make police leave and you must fear RP if police arrive and scatter.
  • Simple things like drive-bys, kidnappings, stabbings etc, fall under the rivalry category.
  • If a scene ends with a shooting there must be 30 minutes from the time the last person is revived before you enter their territory again to start another scene or initiate another conflict.

Gang War

Gang Wars must follow a natural evolution of roleplay. Something crucial within RP must happen between the two parties in order for a war to happen. In most cases the request for peace must be accepted unless there is a very good reason to push for more war; however, peace can come at a cost.

  • A gang support ticket must be opened for the initiating gang to get war approval.
  • To start a gang war an in-city parley must happen with The Elite Order present to discuss the terms of the war. At this time violence must not occur.
  • The Elite order will issue flags to both gangs, whichever gang collects the majority of the opposing gangs’ flags, wins.
  • After someone is downed for any reason you must sit out the rest of that round.
  • Gang wars become on sight with minimal interaction.
  • If rules are broken, the war gets excessively toxic or goes federal, the war will be stopped and the gang with the most flags wins.
  • Gangs can only be in one war at a time. A two-week waiting period must be waited before the initiating can declare war on the same gang again. There is no cooldown for initiating war with another gang, but must have a valid reason with supporting RP to warrant a war.
  • Gangs must conduct gang wars in their respective colors. Anyone not in Gang colors must either leave and not come back during the scene, or surrender. If vehicles bearing the color of an attacking/retaliating gang enter a gang turf, they are subject to being fired upon.
  • Only members on your roster can be involved in the war, this does not include hang-arounds.

Gang Member Scene Limits

Unrecognized Gang

  • 5 members allowed for gang on gang interactions
  • 4 members +1 lookout for criminal interactions

Recognized and Elite Gangs

  • 7 Members for gang on gang interactions
  • 6 members + 1 lookout for criminal interactions
  • If you are pushed on your BLOCK you can use all your members. This does not include any territory you have taken over, it must be the block you registered with Franklin and The Elite Order.
  • Members that are not verified on your roster and the DRP Gang Discord CANNOT flag your gang or be included in gang activity as a member.

If your gang violates any of the gang rules / guidelines, you will be subject to in-city fines and/or suspensions.
